Workflow

Client jobs, platform orchestration, and worker delivery

The system moves from job request through worker assignment, work delivery, quality checks, and final payout while keeping everyone informed.

Step 1: Post & Discover

Clients post jobs, freelancers browse listings, and the matching engine surfaces ideal candidates.

Step 2: Assign & Track

The job management service assigns talent, tracks progress, and logs milestones.

Step 3: Review & Pay

QA verifies work before approval, then the payment gateway processes payments and payouts.
